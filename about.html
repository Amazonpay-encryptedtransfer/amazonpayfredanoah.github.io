About Us

Welcome to Amazon Pay, an initiative powered by Amazon in collaboration with Hugh Jackman, designed to provide support, resources, and recovery for individuals impacted by online scams. With the rise of digital fraud, we are committed to empowering and educating our community to stay safe online, while offering support to those who have already been affected.

Our Mission

Our mission is clear: to help victims of online scams regain control, rebuild their lives, and foster a safer digital environment for all. Through a trusted partnership with Amazon Pay and the dedication of Hugh Jackman, we are raising awareness, building a network of resources, and providing actionable tools to help prevent fraud and support those in need.

Why We’re Here

At Amazon Pay, we understand the importance of trust in every transaction. Scams not only affect individuals financially but can also have lasting emotional impacts. That’s why we are teaming up with Hugh Jackman, who shares our commitment to protecting and uplifting our community. Together, we’re here to make a difference by sharing resources, inspiring resilience, and taking a stand against online fraud.

What We’re Offering

We offer financial and legal support to victims of online scams, including resources for legal assistance and recovery strategies.
